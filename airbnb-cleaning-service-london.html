Superhost Tips

What London Superhosts do differently

Airbnb's Superhost badge requires a minimum 4.8 overall rating — and cleanliness is one of the five rating categories guests score separately. Hosts who consistently score 5 stars on cleanliness share a few common habits.

  • They use the same cleaner every time. Guests notice when the property looks different between stays. Consistency in who cleans means consistency in how it looks.
  • They don't cut the turnaround time. Rushing a clean to fit a same-day check-in is the fastest way to miss something. Build 30 minutes of buffer into your window.
  • They have a written checklist. Not a mental list — a written, room-by-room checklist that gets checked off every single clean. We work to one by default.
  • They restock between every stay. Running out of toilet paper or hand soap is a 1-star review waiting to happen. Restocking should be automatic, not optional.
  • They inspect before check-in. The best hosts — or their cleaners — do a final walkthrough from the guest's perspective before every check-in. We do this as part of every clean.

FAQs

Common questions from London Airbnb hosts

How much does Airbnb cleaning cost in London?
Airbnb turnaround cleaning starts from £65 for a studio or one-bedroom in London, £95 for two bedrooms, and £135 for three bedrooms. Larger properties are quoted individually. Prices include the full clean, linen change using your linen, and completion photos. Restocking and linen hire are available as add-ons.
How quickly can you turn around a clean between guests?
A studio or one-bedroom can typically be turned around in 2–3 hours. Two-bedroom properties take 3–4 hours. We work to your check-out and check-in window — if you have a tight gap between guests, tell us and we'll plan accordingly or send a two-person team.
Do you do same-day Airbnb cleans?
Yes — for regular clients and where availability allows. Same-day bookings should be placed before 9am where possible. Call us directly on 020 8073 0621 for urgent requests and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Do you provide linen for Airbnb properties?
We can manage linen changes using your own linen stock as standard. If you'd like us to supply hotel-grade linen or manage a laundry service, this is available at additional cost. Many of our regular hosts use a par stock system — we'll explain how this works when you get in touch.
Can you restock toiletries and consumables?
Yes. We can check and restock items including toilet paper, hand soap, washing-up liquid, bin bags, and basic guest welcome items. You supply the stock and we top up from what's on-site. If anything is running low, we'll let you know so you can reorder.
Do I need to be at the property for the clean?
No — you don't need to be present. Most of our Airbnb clients use a key safe or smart lock. We arrive at checkout, complete the clean, and send you photo confirmation when done. All our staff are DBS checked and fully insured.
What happens if I have a last-minute booking?
Let us know as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate. Regular clients get priority for last-minute bookings. For same-day requests, call us directly on 020 8073 0621 rather than submitting a form — it's faster.
