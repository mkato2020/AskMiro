Leadership
Mike Kato
Co-Founder · AskMiro Cleaning Services

Mike founded AskMiro with a straightforward goal: to give London businesses a cleaning partner they could genuinely rely on — not just a contractor who shows up and disappears.

Under his leadership, AskMiro has grown into a fully managed cleaning operation covering commercial offices, residential blocks, specialist sectors and everything in between across London.

Every AskMiro contract includes a dedicated account manager, regular quality audits and a direct line to the team — because accountability matters as much as the clean itself.
