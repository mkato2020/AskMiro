FAQs

Common questions.

How do you price your cleaning contracts?

+

We provide fixed monthly rates based on your site, frequency, and scope. After a site visit, we send a detailed quote showing exactly what's included. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Are your staff vetted and insured?

+

Yes. All staff are reference-checked, DBS-checked where required, and trained before deployment. We carry £10M public liability insurance and can provide certificates on request.

What areas do you cover?

+

We primarily serve London and the South East. For larger contracts, we can cover other UK regions. Get in touch and we'll confirm coverage for your location.

How quickly can you start?

+

For most contracts, we can begin within 5–10 working days of signing. Emergency or urgent mobilisation is sometimes possible — contact us directly to discuss.

Do you provide COSHH documentation?

+

Yes. COSHH risk assessments, method statements (RAMS), and safety data sheets are provided as standard for every site. We're audit-ready.

What happens if a cleaner is absent?

+

We provide trained cover who has been briefed on your site. You get the service you're paying for, every time. No gaps, no last-minute cancellations.
