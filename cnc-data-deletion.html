Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: 16 April 2026

Key fact: Crown n Cradle does not collect personal data from Facebook users. Our Meta app is used exclusively to publish our own content to our own Facebook Page. We do not store user profile data, friends lists, messages, or any personally identifiable information.

What data we hold

The only data passing through our systems that relates to a Facebook user is their public comments on our own Page — and those comments are stored by Facebook, not by us. We do not retain our own copy.

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Email

Send an email to crownandcradleofficial@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: Data Deletion Request
  • Body: Your Facebook username or profile URL, plus any specific content you want removed

We will respond within 14 days.

Option 2 — Facebook message

Send a direct message to our Facebook Page with the same information.

Option 3 — Remove your own comments

If your concern is a comment you left on one of our posts, you can delete it yourself through Facebook. We have no independent copy.

Confirmation

We will provide a Confirmation ID in the format CNC-DEL-YYYYMMDD-NNN for your records.

If you are dissatisfied

  • UK Information Commissioner's Office: ico.org.uk
  • Meta: facebook.com/help
