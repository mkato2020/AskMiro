Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16 April 2026 · Last updated: 16 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Crown n Cradle ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our parenting content channel and the automation software we operate to publish that content to our own social media accounts.

1. Who we are

Crown n Cradle is a parenting content channel serving parents of children aged 0–3. We publish short-form video content across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest.

Contact: crownandcradleofficial@gmail.com

2. What this policy covers

This policy covers our use of the Meta (Facebook) Graph API and other social media APIs to publish our own content to our own Pages and accounts.

Importantly: Crown n Cradle does not operate a consumer-facing product. We do not ask users to log in to our software. The Graph API access we hold is used solely to publish content to the Facebook Page we own and operate.

3. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect personal data from any Facebook user
  • We do not ask viewers to authenticate with our software
  • We do not read, store, or share Facebook users' personal data, friends lists, messages, or profile information
  • We do not use any Meta Pixel, tracking pixel, or third-party analytics that identifies individual viewers
  • We do not sell, rent, or share data with advertisers or data brokers

4. Information we do handle

a) Our own access tokens. Stored locally on our development machine, not publicly accessible, not transmitted to any third party.

b) Content we author and publish. Videos, captions, hashtags, and pinned comments we publish to our own Facebook Page.

c) Upload logs. Publish time, resulting post ID, and title — for tracking which videos have been posted. Contains no personal data about any Facebook user.

5. Comments and replies from viewers

When viewers leave comments on our Facebook Page, those comments are visible to us through the standard Facebook Page interface. We do not export, store, or redistribute comments left by viewers.

6. Data retention

  • Access tokens: retained until revoked or expired
  • Upload logs: retained indefinitely for operational history
  • Our own published content: retained until we delete it from our Page

We retain no data from Facebook users beyond what is visible in the public comment thread on our own posts.

7. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or license any data with third parties. The only parties that receive data are:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — when we call the Graph API to publish content
  • Temporary file hosts (e.g. tmpfiles.org) — used briefly during the upload chain, then expired

8. Your rights

If you believe we hold any data about you, contact crownandcradleofficial@gmail.com and we will respond within 14 days.

See our Data Deletion Instructions for the process.

9. Security

Access tokens are stored in files excluded from version control. All API calls are made over HTTPS.

10. Children's privacy

Our content is created about raising children but is intended for their parents. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent change.
